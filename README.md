# classicmodels-sql-analysis
# Classic Models Sales Analysis Using SQL

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the Classic Models database using SQL to generate business insights related to customers, products, sales representatives, offices, inventory, and revenue performance.

The analysis was performed by solving 23 business-oriented SQL case studies categorized into Easy, Medium, and Advanced levels. The project demonstrates practical application of SQL for business reporting and decision-making.

---

## Database Used

**Classic Models Database**

The database contains information about:

* Customers
* Orders
* Order Details
* Products
* Product Lines
* Payments
* Employees
* Offices

## Key Findings

### Customer Insights

* Euro+ Shopping Channel was identified as the highest-value customer, generating **$820,689.54** in total revenue.
* Medium Risk customers represented the largest customer segment with approximately **50 customers**.

### Sales Performance Insights

* Gerard Hernandez emerged as the top-performing sales representative, generating **$1,258,577.81** in revenue.
* Paris office achieved the highest revenue contribution of **$3,083,761.58**.

### Product Insights

* Classic Cars was the highest revenue-generating product line with total sales of **$38,538,922.49**.
* 1961 Chevrolet Impala recorded the highest profit margin of **150.05%**.
* 2002 Suzuki XREO maintained the highest remaining inventory with **8,969 units** available.

### Revenue & Market Insights

* November 2013 generated the highest monthly revenue of **$988,025.15**.
* USA was the strongest performing market with total revenue of **$3,273,280.05**.
